CharacterStream

public final class CharacterStream : Sequence, IteratorProtocol

Represents a buffered stream of Characters backed by an InputStream.

Implemented as a wrapper on InputStream. The chief advantage here over a regular InputStream is that we can peek() and we get a little more Swift-friendly Characters rather than bytes.
